Methodology

Brookhart Labs’ Side-Quest Generator identifies small, buildable project ideas by scanning public developer signals where unmet needs surface organically.

Inputs include developer discussions, issue threads, roadmap comments, and social posts that express friction, missing functionality, or unrealized opportunities. These signals are filtered for specificity, feasibility, and relevance to builders.

Each idea is synthesized into a concise “side quest” with a clearly defined problem, target audience, and implementation scope. Ideas are intentionally constrained to remain achievable by individuals or small teams.

This project prioritizes cultural signal over market timing and does not attempt to predict financial outcomes. It is a research and ideation tool, not investment advice.
